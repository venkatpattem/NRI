CSE

Computer Science Engineering is a 4 year programme, which emphasises the basics of computer programming and networking, comprises a very large amount of something.

ECE

Electronics and Communications Engineering (ECE) is a 4year programme which involves researching, designing, developing and testing of electronic equipment used in various systems.

EEE

Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE), deals with the engineering problems, opportunities and needs of electrical, electronics, computer, telecommunication systems and related industries.

MECH

Mechanical engineering (with 4years) is a branch that combines engineering physics and mathematics principles with materials science to design, analyze, manufacture, and maintain mechanical systems.

CIVIL

Civil engineering(4year course) is a professional engineering discipline that deals with the design, construction, and maintenance of the physical and naturally built environment, including public works such as roads,railways and etc.

MINING

The branch of engineering dealing with the location and appraisal of mineral deposits and the laying out, equipment, and management of mines with 4year duration.

AERONAUTICAL

The branch of engineering concerned with the design, production, and maintenance of aircraft which is a 4-year undergraduated programme.Courses in aeronautics prepare candidates for jobs in the field of aerospace and aviation.

AEROSPACE

Aerospace Engineering is a branch of Engineering that provides skills and knowledge to design, manufacture and maintain spacecrafts, aircrafts, missiles and weapons systems.

AUTOMOBILE

Automobile Engineering is a branch of vehicle engineering which includes elements of mechanical, electrical, electronic, manufacture and operation of motorcycles,buses and trucks and their respective engineering subsystems.

BIOCHEMICAL

The study in Biochemical Engineering focuses on the outline and construction of unit processes that involve biology, chemistry, molecules or engineering knowledge - it includes everything from raw materials preparation to product recovery.

BIOMEDICAL

Biomedical engineering focuses on the advances that improve human health and health care at all levels.Biomedical engineering is the application of the principles and problem-solving techniques of engineering to biology and medicine.

BIOTECHNOLOGY

Biotechnology engineering is the research and study of micro-organism, bio-organisms, cell functions in living beings. It is basically the blend of both Biological science and technology.

CONSTRUCTION

Construction engineering is a professional discipline that deals with designing, planning, construction and management of infrastructures such as roads, tunnels, bridges, airports, railroads, facilities, buildings, dams, utilities and other projects.

CHEMICAL

Chemical engineering is the branch of engineering that deals with chemical production and the manufacture of products through chemical processes. This includes designing equipment, systems and processes for refining raw materials and for mixing to make valuable products.

AGRICULTURE & FARM

The branch of engineering involved with the design of farm machinery, with soil management, land development, and mechanization and automation of livestock farming, and with the efficient planting, harvesting, storage, and processing of farm commodities.

DAIRY

Dairy technology is a division of engineering that deals with the processing of milk and its products. Dairy technology study involves processing, storage, distribution and transportation of dairy products by implying the science of bacteriology, nutrition and biochemistry.

CERAMIC

Ceramic Technology is an emerging and developing branch of engineering that deals with the study of the manufacture, properties, design and uses of ceramic related things.

ENVIRONMENTAL

Environmental Engineering is a very popular discipline of engineering that deals with the issues related to the environment.It is a 4year undergraduated course.

FOOD

Food Technology is a science branch that deals with the techniques involved in production, processing, preservation, packaging, labeling, quality management, and distribution of food products.

GENETIC

Genetic Engineering additionally called genetic modification.Genetic Engineering has been connected in various fields including research, medicine, industrial biotechnology and agriculture.

INDUSTRIAL

Industrial engineering is an engineering profession that is concerned with the optimization of complex processes, systems, or organizations by developing, improving and implementing integrated systems of people, money, knowledge, information and equipment.

INFORMATION

Information Technology (IT) has become the most fundamental need for proper functioning of human society.Information Technology is everywhere in our daily lives from building a spaceship to delivering food at doorsteps.

INSTRUMENTATION

Industrial Instrumentation Technology is the installation, maintenance and calibration of devices used in the automation of industrial processes. These devices measure and control the pressure, temperature, level and flow of processes used in automated manufacturing and production.

MARINE

Marine engineering is a branch of engineering that deals with the construction as well as the operation of mechanical equipment of seagoing craft, docks, and harbor installations.

MATERIAL SCIENCE

Materials Science (also referred to as materials science and engineering) is an inter-disciplinary subject that involves the study of all materials starting from a glass or a piece of sport equipment to those used in medicine and aerospace.

MECHATRONICS

Mechatronics engineering is a multidisciplinary study of the engineering/technology field that deals with the study of electronics engineering, mechanical engineering, telecommunications, computer engineering, system engineering, and control engineering.

METALLURGICAL

Metallurgy is a domain of materials science and engineering that studies the physical and chemical behavior of metallic elements, their inter-metallic compounds, and their mixtures, which are called alloys.

NANOTECNOLOGY

The study of Nanotechnology involves the utilization of various fields of science. Such fields include organic chemistry, microfabrication, molecular biology, atomic science, surface science, and semiconductor material science.

NAVAL ARCHITECTURE and SHIP BUILDING

Naval architecture is an engineering discipline incorporating elements of mechanical, electrical, electronic, software and safety engineering as applied to the engineering design process, shipbuilding, maintenance, and operation of marine vessels and structures.

NAVAL ARCITECTURE and OCEAN ENGINEERING

The ocean engineers get to know the details about the ocean policies and the areas of concern for modern navigators as well as the technological requirements for further establishment of engineering wonders across the seas

NUCLEAR

Nuclear engineers apply knowledge of atomic nuclei to design, build, and operate special equipment that is useful to humanity. These applications fall into several broad categories, including fission power, radiation detection, nuclear medicine, nuclear materials, and plasma science.

PETROLEUM

Petroleum Engineering is related to the innovation and exploration of the process of oil and gas extraction. It is advanced from Mining Engineering and Geology, and linked to Geoscience.

PHARMACEUTICAL

Pharmaceutical engineering focuses on designing, building, and improving manufacturing facilities that produce drugs. Although some pharma engineers also work with discovering and formulating medication.

POLYMER

Polymer Technology can be described in brief as the manufacture, processing, analysis and application of long chain molecules. Materials that are typically classified as polymers include: plastics, paints, rubber, foams, adhesives, sealants, varnishes and many more.

POWER

Power engineering, also called power systems engineering, is a subfield of electrical engineering that deals with the generation, transmission, distribution, and utilization of electric power.

PRODUCTION

Production Engineering, also known as Manufacture Engineering, deals with planning, designing, developing and managing of various processes to produce high quality products.

ROBOTICS

Robotics is an interdisciplinary field that integrates computer science and engineering.Robotics involves design, construction, operation, and use of robots. The goal of robotics is to design machines that can help and assist humans.

TELECOMMUNICATION

Telecommunications Engineering is an engineering discipline centered on electrical and computer engineering which seeks to support and enhance telecommunication systems.The work ranges from basic circuit design to strategic mass developments.
