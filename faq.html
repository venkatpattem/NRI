Q: WHAT IS NRIQUOTA.COM

NRIQUOTA.COM is a career guidance platform which creates awareness to the students for achieving success in their higher education career.

Q: What kind of services will be availed from NRIQUOTA.COM

Counseling and career guidance.

Support for choosing better course and better opportunities.

Application registration, choice filling, attending different states counseling schedules.

Guidance provided by highly experienced and well qualified faculty to achieve great heights in the chosen career.

Online and offline counseling for improving the communication and soft skills.

Q: How many rounds of counselling will be conducted for Deemed and Central Universities?
The authorities will conduct two rounds of counselling followed by a mop-up round for Deemed/Central if seats remain vacant.
Q: What is the Counseling process of NEET?
The registration is done in an online mode. To complete this process, candidates are required to their NEET UG application number, Date of Birth, their name and roll number. The process is concluded with the final submission after completing the 'Choice Filling' option.
Q: What are the things required for NEET Counselling?Documents Required for NEET Counselling 2020 MBBS Admission

1.MBBS admission application form in the prescribed format.

2. NEET Hall Ticket or Admit Card.

3. NEET Rank Card.

4. NEET Score Card.

5. Date of Birth (D.O.B.) Certificate.

6. Provisional Seat Allotment Letter/ Call Letter.

7. Cast Certificate.

8. Prepaid Fee Receipt.

Q: How can guidance help the students?

Maintain academic standards and set goals for academic success. Develop skills to improve organization, study habits, and time management. Work through personal problems that may affect academics or relationships. Improve social skills.

Q: What are the 4 components of Counselling?

The basic stages of counseling are:

1) Developing the client/clinician relationship;

2) Clarifying and assessing the presenting problem or situation;

3) Identifying and setting counseling or treatment goals;

4) Designing and implementing interventions; and

5) Planning, termination, and follow-up.
